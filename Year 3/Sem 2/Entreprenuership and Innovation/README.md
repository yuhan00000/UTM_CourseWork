Entrepreneurship and Innovation introduced the principles of innovation, opportunity recognition, business model development, and entrepreneurial thinking. The course encouraged students to view technology not only as a technical solution but also as a tool for creating value and addressing real-world challenges.

Through discussions, case studies, and project activities, students gained exposure to the process of transforming ideas into viable products and services. The course emphasized creativity, problem-solving, leadership, and adaptability as important competencies for both entrepreneurs and technology professionals.

One of the key lessons learned was that successful innovation requires a deep understanding of user needs, market demands, and sustainable business practices. Exposure to entrepreneurial concepts broadened perspectives beyond technical development and highlighted the importance of aligning innovation with practical value creation.

Overall, the course strengthened strategic thinking and encouraged a proactive mindset toward identifying opportunities for technological innovation and business growth.
